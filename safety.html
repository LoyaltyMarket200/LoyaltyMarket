Safety Tips

Learn how to buy, sell, communicate, and meet other users more safely when using LoyaltyMarket.

On This Page

Important: LoyaltyMarket connects buyers and sellers, but users are responsible for checking products, people, documents, payments, and meeting locations before completing a transaction.

1. General Safety

  • Use common sense and do not rush into a transaction.
  • Ask questions before agreeing to buy or sell anything.
  • Check the seller's profile, product details, and photographs.
  • Keep important communication inside LoyaltyMarket when possible.
  • Do not share passwords, verification codes, or banking passwords.
  • Stop communicating if someone pressures or threatens you.
  • Report suspicious advertisements, accounts, or messages.
Best Practice: A genuine buyer or seller should allow you enough time to inspect, ask questions, and make a careful decision.

2. Safety Tips for Buyers

  • Read the full advertisement before contacting the seller.
  • Compare the price with similar products.
  • Be careful when a price is far lower than normal.
  • Ask for recent and clear photographs of the actual item.
  • Inspect the item before paying whenever possible.
  • Test phones, electronics, appliances, and machines.
  • Check serial numbers, receipts, ownership documents, or warranties.
  • Do not send a deposit to an unknown person without verification.
  • Ask for proof that the seller has the legal right to sell the item.
Never purchase an item that you believe may be stolen, counterfeit, illegal, damaged beyond what was disclosed, or owned by someone else.

3. Safety Tips for Sellers

  • Use accurate descriptions and recent photographs.
  • Clearly disclose defects, damage, or missing parts.
  • Confirm that payment is complete before releasing an item.
  • Do not rely only on screenshots or payment messages.
  • Check your real account balance directly.
  • Do not give buyers your password or verification code.
  • Be cautious of buyers offering more than your asking price.
  • Do not accept suspicious requests involving refunds or third parties.
  • Keep records of important sales and conversations.
A payment screenshot does not prove that money entered your account. Always verify the payment yourself before handing over the item.

4. Payment Safety

Use payment methods that you understand and can verify independently. LoyaltyMarket may not control payments arranged directly between users.

  • Confirm the amount, currency, and recipient before paying.
  • Do not share bank login details or mobile-money PINs.
  • Do not send verification codes to another user.
  • Be careful of fake payment links and fake websites.
  • Avoid unusual payment requests involving gift cards or strangers.
  • Keep payment receipts and transaction references.
  • Verify refunds through the original payment channel.
Currency Reminder: Confirm whether the advertised price is in United States Dollars or Liberian Dollars before making payment.

5. Meeting Other Users Safely

  • Meet during daylight hours whenever possible.
  • Choose a busy public location.
  • Tell a trusted person where you are going.
  • Bring a trusted person with you for expensive transactions.
  • Keep your phone charged and available.
  • Avoid isolated areas and private locations with strangers.
  • Do not carry unnecessary amounts of cash.
  • Leave immediately if the situation feels unsafe.
For high-value items, consider meeting near a bank, police station, established business, or another secure public location.

6. Common Scam Warning Signs

  • The price is unrealistically low.
  • The person refuses inspection or a public meeting.
  • The person pressures you to pay immediately.
  • The seller requests a deposit before providing proof.
  • The buyer claims to have sent too much money.
  • The person sends suspicious links.
  • The person asks for your verification code.
  • The photographs appear copied from another website.
  • The story or product details keep changing.
  • The seller cannot prove ownership.
  • The person asks you to communicate only outside LoyaltyMarket.
Stop the transaction if someone asks you to pay money before you have enough information to verify the person, product, and payment request.

7. Protect Your LoyaltyMarket Account

  • Create a strong and unique password.
  • Do not use the same password everywhere.
  • Never share your password with another person.
  • Do not share account verification codes.
  • Log out when using a shared computer or phone.
  • Be careful of fake LoyaltyMarket messages.
  • Check the website address before entering login information.
  • Report unauthorized account access immediately.
Remember: LoyaltyMarket support should never ask you to send your password, banking password, mobile-money PIN, or private verification code.

8. Vehicles, Property, and High-Value Items

Vehicles

  • Inspect and test-drive the vehicle safely.
  • Use a qualified mechanic when possible.
  • Check the chassis number and ownership documents.
  • Confirm registration, taxes, and legal ownership.
  • Do not pay fully before confirming the documents.

Land and Property

  • Verify the owner's identity and legal authority.
  • Inspect the location in person.
  • Check deeds, titles, surveys, and supporting documents.
  • Confirm that the property is not under dispute.
  • Use a qualified lawyer or property professional.
  • Do not rely only on photographs or verbal promises.
Vehicle, land, and property transactions can involve serious legal and financial risks. Obtain independent professional advice before transferring large amounts of money.

9. Prohibited and Dangerous Items

Do not use LoyaltyMarket to advertise, buy, or sell illegal, dangerous, stolen, counterfeit, or prohibited items.

  • Stolen property.
  • Counterfeit goods.
  • Illegal drugs or controlled substances.
  • Weapons or dangerous materials prohibited by law.
  • Fraudulent documents.
  • Items that violate intellectual-property rights.
  • Products that create an unreasonable safety risk.
  • Other items prohibited by LoyaltyMarket's Terms of Use.

10. Reporting Suspicious Activity

Report advertisements, accounts, messages, scams, fraud, impersonation, harassment, illegal content, or safety concerns.

Include useful information such as the advertisement title, username, phone number, screenshots, payment records, and a clear explanation of what happened.

11. Emergencies and Criminal Activity

LoyaltyMarket is not an emergency service. If you are in immediate danger, move to a safe location and contact the appropriate local authorities.

Suspected theft, fraud, violence, threats, or other criminal activity may also be reported to law-enforcement authorities.

Do not attempt to confront a suspected criminal alone. Protect yourself first and preserve useful evidence for the authorities.
