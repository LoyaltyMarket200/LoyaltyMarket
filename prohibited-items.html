LoyaltyMarket Marketplace Policy

Prohibited Items

These products, services, and activities are not allowed on LoyaltyMarket. Listings that violate this policy may be removed without notice.

Important: Users must follow applicable Liberian laws and LoyaltyMarket rules. A product or service may be prohibited even when it is not specifically named on this page.

1. Illegal, Stolen, or Unlawfully Obtained Items

LoyaltyMarket does not allow listings for items that are illegal, stolen, unlawfully obtained, or connected to criminal activity.

  • Stolen phones, vehicles, electronics, or personal property.
  • Items with removed, altered, or hidden serial numbers.
  • Property obtained through fraud or deception.
  • Items seized or restricted by lawful authorities.
  • Goods that a seller does not have legal authority to sell.
  • Products imported, transported, or sold illegally.
LoyaltyMarket may preserve relevant information and cooperate with lawful investigations involving suspected stolen property.

2. Weapons, Ammunition, and Explosives

Weapons and dangerous devices are not allowed unless LoyaltyMarket later introduces a specifically authorized and legally compliant category.

  • Firearms and firearm parts.
  • Ammunition and bullets.
  • Explosives, grenades, bombs, or explosive materials.
  • Silencers and restricted firearm accessories.
  • Switchblades or prohibited combat weapons.
  • Tasers, stun weapons, or other restricted devices.
  • Instructions for building weapons or explosives.
Listings involving weapons or explosives may be removed immediately and may be reported where required by law.

3. Illegal Drugs and Controlled Substances

  • Illegal drugs and narcotics.
  • Controlled substances sold without authorization.
  • Drug paraphernalia intended for illegal drug use.
  • Prescription medicine sold without lawful authority.
  • Substances falsely advertised as legal alternatives to drugs.
  • Ingredients or equipment clearly intended for drug production.
Ordinary lawful pharmacy products may require additional verification, licensing, or marketplace approval before being listed.

4. Restricted Medical and Health Products

Some health products may create serious risks when sold without professional oversight or legal authorization.

  • Prescription-only medicines without proper authorization.
  • Controlled or restricted medications.
  • Unsafe, recalled, expired, or contaminated medical products.
  • Human organs, blood, tissue, or bodily materials.
  • False cures or misleading medical treatments.
  • Unlicensed medical procedures.
  • Products making dangerous or unsupported health claims.
Lawful pharmacies and qualified medical providers may need separate verification before advertising approved products or services.

5. Counterfeit, Fake, and Pirated Goods

  • Fake designer clothing, shoes, bags, or watches.
  • Counterfeit electronics or accessories.
  • Pirated movies, music, software, or games.
  • Fake brand labels, packaging, or certificates.
  • Unauthorized copies of copyrighted products.
  • Products falsely presented as genuine.
Sellers must clearly state when an item is generic, compatible, refurbished, or not manufactured by the original brand.

6. Government Documents and Personal Information

  • Passports, visas, residence permits, or identity cards.
  • Driver's licenses or government-issued certificates.
  • Bank cards, SIM cards registered to another person, or account access.
  • Stolen personal records or private databases.
  • Login credentials, passwords, verification codes, or PINs.
  • Fake academic certificates or professional licenses.
  • Forged legal documents, deeds, receipts, or permits.
Do not publicly upload identification documents, banking information, or other sensitive personal records to a listing.

7. Adult Services and Explicit Content

  • Prostitution or paid sexual services.
  • Pornographic or sexually explicit content.
  • Sexual content involving minors.
  • Escort services offered for sexual purposes.
  • Non-consensual intimate photographs or videos.
  • Services involving sexual exploitation or trafficking.
Content involving the sexual exploitation of children is strictly prohibited and may be reported to the appropriate authorities.

8. Protected Animals and Wildlife Products

  • Protected, endangered, or illegally captured animals.
  • Wildlife products prohibited by law.
  • Ivory, illegal skins, horns, teeth, or animal parts.
  • Animals offered for fighting or cruel activity.
  • Animals sold in unsafe or inhumane conditions.
  • Products made from protected wildlife.
Permitted animal listings must follow health, welfare, ownership, transport, and local legal requirements.

9. Financial Products, Gambling, and Money Scams

  • Stolen bank accounts or financial credentials.
  • Fake investment opportunities or guaranteed-profit schemes.
  • Pyramid schemes and unlawful recruitment programs.
  • Money-laundering services.
  • Fake loans or advance-fee loan scams.
  • Counterfeit currency.
  • Illegal gambling services or devices.
  • Requests to transfer or receive suspicious funds.
LoyaltyMarket must not be used to move, hide, disguise, or obtain money through fraud or unlawful activity.

10. Dangerous, Toxic, or Hazardous Materials

  • Radioactive materials.
  • Toxic chemicals or poisons.
  • Restricted pesticides.
  • Unsafe industrial chemicals.
  • Biological hazards.
  • Dangerous substances without proper labeling or permits.
  • Recalled products that create serious safety risks.
Lawful commercial chemicals may require licensing, secure handling, accurate labeling, and marketplace approval.

11. Prohibited Services and Activities

  • Hacking, spyware, malware, or unauthorized account access.
  • Fake document creation.
  • Exam cheating or selling stolen test materials.
  • Human trafficking or exploitation.
  • Paid violence, threats, or intimidation.
  • Unlicensed legal, financial, or medical services where prohibited.
  • Services that promote fraud, theft, or illegal activity.
  • Identity theft or impersonation services.

12. Enforcement

LoyaltyMarket may review, reject, restrict, or remove listings that violate this policy or create legal, financial, or safety risks.

Depending on the situation, LoyaltyMarket may:

  • Remove or reject a listing.
  • Request proof of ownership or authorization.
  • Limit account features.
  • Suspend or permanently remove an account.
  • Preserve evidence connected to a report.
  • Cooperate with lawful government or police requests.
Attempting to avoid enforcement by changing descriptions, using coded words, or creating another account is also prohibited.

13. Report a Prohibited Listing

Report any listing that appears illegal, stolen, dangerous, counterfeit, fraudulent, or otherwise prohibited.

Include the listing title, seller name, screenshots, links, and a clear explanation of the concern.
