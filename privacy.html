Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Waterside collects, uses, stores, and protects information when people use our marketplace.

Effective date: July 13, 2026

By using Waterside, you acknowledge that your information may be processed as described in this Privacy Policy. Please read this page carefully before creating an account, posting an advertisement, contacting another user, or completing a marketplace transaction.

1. Introduction

Waterside is an online marketplace designed to help individuals and businesses in Liberia advertise, discover, buy, sell, and discuss products and services.

This Privacy Policy applies to the Waterside website, mobile applications, services, features, and communications operated by Waterside.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We may collect information that you provide when creating an account, completing your profile, posting an advertisement, contacting support, or communicating with another user.

  • Your full name or business name.
  • Email address and telephone number.
  • Account password and login information.
  • Profile photo or business logo.
  • Product photographs, descriptions, prices, and locations.
  • Messages and communication sent through the platform.
  • Reports, complaints, feedback, and support requests.

Information Collected Automatically

When you use Waterside, certain technical information may be collected automatically.

  • Internet Protocol address.
  • Browser and device type.
  • Operating system.
  • Pages visited and features used.
  • Date, time, and duration of activity.
  • Approximate location based on device or network information.

3. How We Use Information

Waterside may use collected information to:

  • Create, maintain, and secure user accounts.
  • Display advertisements and seller information.
  • Connect buyers, sellers, and service providers.
  • Enable messages and marketplace communication.
  • Improve search results and user experience.
  • Detect fraud, scams, spam, abuse, and prohibited activity.
  • Investigate reports and enforce our Terms of Use.
  • Send account, safety, support, and service notifications.
  • Comply with lawful requests and legal obligations.
  • Improve and develop Waterside services.

4. Information Sharing

Waterside does not sell users' personal information. Information may be shared only when necessary to operate, secure, improve, or legally protect the platform.

Information may be shared with:

  • Other users when you publish an advertisement or choose to share your contact information.
  • Service providers that assist with hosting, storage, security, communication, analytics, or technical support.
  • Law-enforcement agencies, courts, regulators, or government authorities when legally required.
  • Professional advisers where necessary to protect Waterside's legal or business interests.
  • A future owner or partner if Waterside is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of business assets.

5. Messages and Transactions

Waterside may store or review marketplace messages when reasonably necessary to provide support, investigate fraud, respond to safety complaints, or enforce platform rules.

Buyers and sellers are responsible for deciding what personal information they share with each other. Users should avoid sharing passwords, verification codes, banking passwords, or other sensitive information.

Unless Waterside introduces an official payment service, transactions and payments arranged between users occur independently of Waterside.

6. Cookies and Similar Technology

Waterside may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to remember user preferences, maintain login sessions, improve performance, understand usage, and protect the platform.

Users may manage cookies through their browser settings. Disabling some cookies may cause certain features to stop working correctly.

7. Data Security

Waterside intends to use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect user information from unauthorized access, alteration, misuse, loss, or disclosure.

However, no website, mobile application, storage system, or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Users are responsible for protecting their passwords, devices, and account access.

Users should immediately report suspected account access, scams, fraud, impersonation, or security incidents.

8. Data Retention

Waterside may retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, operate accounts, resolve disputes, investigate abuse, prevent fraud, comply with legal obligations, and enforce platform agreements.

Some information may remain in backups, safety records, transaction records, or legal records after an account is deleted where retention is reasonably necessary.

9. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on applicable law and available platform features, users may request to:

  • Review or correct account information.
  • Update profile and contact details.
  • Remove or edit advertisements.
  • Change communication preferences.
  • Request account deletion.
  • Ask questions about personal information.
  • Report suspected misuse of personal information.

Waterside may require identity or account verification before completing certain privacy or account requests.

10. Children's Privacy

Waterside is not intended for children who are unable to legally enter into an agreement. Minors may use the platform only with appropriate permission and supervision from a parent or legal guardian.

Parents or guardians who believe a child has submitted personal information without appropriate permission should contact Waterside.

11. External Links and Services

Waterside advertisements or messages may contain links to websites, payment providers, social-media platforms, or services operated by third parties.

Waterside does not control the privacy practices, security, content, or services of independent third parties. Users should review the privacy policies of those services before providing information.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Waterside may update this Privacy Policy as the platform, technology, legal requirements, or business practices change.

The effective date shown at the top of this page will be updated when important changes are made. Continued use of Waterside after an update means that the revised policy will apply to future use of the platform.

13. Contact Us

Questions, privacy requests, complaints, or reports about personal information may be sent through Waterside's official support channels.
