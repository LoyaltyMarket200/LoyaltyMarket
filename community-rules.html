Waterside Community Standards

Community Rules

These rules help keep Waterside respectful, trustworthy, useful, and safe for buyers, sellers, businesses, and service providers.

Everyone using Waterside is expected to act honestly, respectfully, and responsibly. These Community Rules apply to listings, profiles, photographs, messages, reviews, reports, and all other activity on Waterside.

1. Respect Other Users

Treat buyers, sellers, service providers, businesses, and support staff with respect.

  • Use respectful and professional language.
  • Allow others enough time to respond.
  • Do not insult, humiliate, intimidate, or provoke other users.
  • Respect another person's decision not to complete a transaction.
  • Do not discriminate against users unfairly.
  • Do not publish another person's private information.
A respectful marketplace creates better opportunities and stronger trust between buyers and sellers.

2. Create Honest and Accurate Listings

Advertisements must truthfully describe the product, service, property, vehicle, job, or business being offered.

  • Use accurate titles, descriptions, prices, and locations.
  • Upload photographs of the actual item whenever possible.
  • Disclose defects, damage, missing parts, or important limitations.
  • Select the correct category and condition.
  • State whether the price is in USD or Liberian Dollars.
  • Do not advertise items that are unavailable.
  • Do not copy another user's listing without permission.
  • Remove or update listings after items are sold.
Misleading descriptions, fake prices, copied photographs, and hidden defects may result in listing removal or account restrictions.

3. Communicate Responsibly

  • Ask clear and relevant questions.
  • Do not pressure another user into making a payment.
  • Do not send abusive or inappropriate messages.
  • Do not repeatedly contact someone who asked you to stop.
  • Do not request passwords, PINs, or verification codes.
  • Do not send suspicious links or files.
  • Do not use messages to promote unrelated businesses.
Keep important discussions about price, condition, meeting location, and payment clear and easy to understand.

4. No Spam or Manipulation

Waterside must not be used to flood users or the platform with repetitive, misleading, or irrelevant content.

  • Do not post the same advertisement repeatedly.
  • Do not create duplicate accounts to increase visibility.
  • Do not use false keywords to appear in unrelated searches.
  • Do not send mass promotional messages.
  • Do not manipulate views, favorites, reviews, or reports.
  • Do not create fake activity to make a listing appear popular.

5. No Fraud, Scams, or Deceptive Conduct

Any attempt to steal money, property, accounts, personal information, or access credentials is prohibited.

  • Do not send fake payment confirmations.
  • Do not claim payment was made when it was not.
  • Do not advertise items you do not own or control.
  • Do not request deposits using false information.
  • Do not use fake identities, receipts, documents, or addresses.
  • Do not create fake job opportunities.
  • Do not trick users into visiting fake websites.
  • Do not participate in refund, overpayment, or delivery scams.
Fraudulent activity may lead to immediate account suspension, permanent removal, and reporting to the appropriate authorities.

6. Identity, Accounts, and Impersonation

  • Provide accurate account information.
  • Do not impersonate another person, business, or organization.
  • Do not pretend to represent Waterside.
  • Do not use another person's photograph without permission.
  • Do not create accounts to avoid suspension.
  • Do not buy, sell, or transfer user accounts.
  • Protect your password and account access.
Verification badges must not be copied, edited, or falsely displayed. Only Waterside may approve official verification.

7. Harassment, Threats, and Harmful Conduct

Waterside does not allow behavior that threatens, intimidates, humiliates, targets, or endangers another person.

  • Do not threaten violence or property damage.
  • Do not sexually harass another user.
  • Do not send hateful or degrading messages.
  • Do not stalk or repeatedly contact another user.
  • Do not encourage violence or self-harm.
  • Do not publish private photographs or information without consent.
  • Do not use Waterside to organize harmful activity.
Serious threats or immediate danger should be reported to the appropriate local authorities.

8. No Illegal or Dangerous Activity

Users must follow applicable laws and may not use Waterside to support illegal or dangerous transactions.

  • Do not advertise stolen property.
  • Do not advertise counterfeit goods.
  • Do not sell illegal drugs or controlled substances.
  • Do not offer fraudulent or forged documents.
  • Do not advertise prohibited weapons or dangerous materials.
  • Do not promote human trafficking or exploitation.
  • Do not advertise services that involve illegal conduct.
  • Do not assist another user in breaking the law.

9. Appropriate Content

Content must be relevant to a legitimate marketplace purpose and suitable for a general audience.

  • Do not upload pornography or sexually explicit content.
  • Do not upload graphic violence.
  • Do not post hateful or discriminatory content.
  • Do not publish personal identification documents publicly.
  • Do not use copyrighted images without permission.
  • Do not post unrelated political or religious arguments.
  • Do not upload malware, harmful code, or suspicious files.
Product images should clearly show the advertised item and must not be used to deceive buyers.

10. Reviews, Ratings, and Feedback

  • Submit honest feedback based on real experiences.
  • Do not create fake positive or negative reviews.
  • Do not exchange money or gifts for dishonest reviews.
  • Do not review your own account or business.
  • Do not include threats or private information in reviews.
  • Do not pressure another user to remove truthful feedback.
Fair reviews help users make more informed marketplace decisions.

11. Enforcement of Community Rules

Waterside may review reports, listings, messages, accounts, and other relevant information when investigating suspected violations.

Depending on the situation, Waterside may:

  • Request additional information.
  • Edit, limit, reject, or remove a listing.
  • Issue a warning.
  • Temporarily restrict account features.
  • Suspend an account.
  • Permanently remove an account.
  • Preserve relevant records.
  • Cooperate with lawful requests from authorities.
Enforcement decisions may consider the seriousness, frequency, risk, evidence, and previous account history connected to a violation.

12. Report Community Rule Violations

Report suspicious listings, abusive messages, fraud, impersonation, harassment, illegal content, fake accounts, or other violations.

Provide useful information such as usernames, listing titles, screenshots, payment records, dates, and a clear explanation.
